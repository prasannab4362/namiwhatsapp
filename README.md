# Nami CRM

![Nami CRM Logo](public/logo.png)

Nami CRM is a next-generation WhatsApp CRM designed to turn your WhatsApp into a sales machine. With No-Code Automations, a Shared Team Inbox, and Sales Pipelines, it allows your business to scale intelligently.
